Taking Care of Old Cats

Senior Cats need Special TLC

Subcutaneous Fluids

Dehydrated kitties can benefit from receiving Subcutaneous(SQ) Fluids.

Appetite Stimulants

Some cats lose interest in food and need gentle appetite stimulants. Transdermal medications make administration less stressful for you and your cat.

Therapeutic Diets

Cats frequently get Chronic Kidney Disease. A therapeutic diet formulated to be easy on the kidneys can prolong your cats life for many months.
